Teen Birth Rates 2014, Ages 15-19

Between 1991 and 2014, the teen birth rate decreased by about 61% nationwide. This decline has taken place in all 50 states and among all racial/ethnic groups. The data below includes teen birth and pregnancy rates among girls between the ages of 15 and 19. Each state is ranked from 1 to 50, which 1 being the lowest number of births, and 50 being the state with the highest number of births.
